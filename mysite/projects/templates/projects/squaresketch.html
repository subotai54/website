Etch-A-Sketch

This is a etch a sketch that creates boards with dimension 1x1 up to 40x40. Each time you hover over a square, it darkens the square by about 10%. Draw to your hearts content! Hitting the reset button will reset the square and let you pick a new dimension if you so choose (default 16). But each time you do it'll come back a different color. If you want your own color, hit the pick color button and format it as shown.
